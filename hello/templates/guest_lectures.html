OUR PREVIOUS SPEAKERS

Prof. Bjarne Stroustrup

Prof. Bjarne Stroustrup
Pre-Technex guest lecture held on 12 January, 2015.Prof. Bjarne Stroustrup a.k.a father of C++ programming language, is a Danish computer scientist. Currently he is working at Morgan Stanley and is also a visiting professor at Columbia University. He has served as the head of AT&T Bell Labs from its creation till 2002. He was elected as a member of National Academy of Engineering in 2004. With numerous notable awards under his name such as Grace Murry Hopper Award, IEEE Computer Society Computer Entrepreneur Award and William Procter Prize for Scientific Achievement, he truly is a gem of the computer world.

Prof. Daniela Cerqui

Prof. Daniela Cerqui
Dr. Daniela Cerqui is a social and cultural anthropologist at the University of Lausanne and explores the edges of "posthuman" society, a society marked by human and computer hybridisation. Her focus is on the development of radical new information technologies and the 'information society' these technologies are supposed to create. She completed a postdoctoral fellowship in the Department of Cybernetics, University of Reading, UK, with Kevin Warwick, a pioneer in the field of brain to machine and brain-to-brain communication and the first person to have a chipimplanted in one of his nerves for enhancement purposes.

Sagarika Ghose

Sagarika Ghose
Sagarika Ghose has been a very noted journalist for over two decades. She received her bachelor's degree in History from St. Stephen's College, Delhi. A recipient of the Rhodes Scholarship in 1987, she was educated at the University of Oxford. In 2014, The Rhodes Project, named her as one of the 13 famous women Rhodes Scholars in the world.She has worked at The Times Of India, Outlook magazine and The Indian Express. She was the deputy editor and a prime time anchor on the news network CNN-IBN. At present, she works as consulting editor at The Times of India. She is the author of two novels, The Gin Drinkers and Blind Faith, both published worldwide by Harper Collins.

Dr. A Sivathanu Pillai

Dr. A Sivathanu Pillai
Dr. A Sivathanu Pillai joined the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) in 1986 and was the Programme Director of the Integrated Guided Missile Development Program under the leadership of Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam. He served as the Chief Controller of R&Dand was a distinguished scientist at DRDO. BrahMos, the India-Russia joint missile development program evolved under his leadership with him as the founder CEO and Managing Director.He has served as the Vice President of International Project Management Association and as Special Secretary representing India in the India-Russia Inter-Governmental Commission on Military-Technical Cooperation.

Rahul Shukla

Rahul Shukla
Rahul Shukla is the Head of Corporate Banking At Citibank India. His team is responsible for full banking coverage of large local Indian corporates, public sector institutions, financial institutions and MNCs operating in India. Rahul is a member of the Global Corporate Banking Operating Committee.Prior to his present assignment, he was Head of the Asia-Pacific TMT (technology, media and telecommunications) Investment Banking team based in Hong Kong.Under his leadership, Citi's TMT franchise was recognised as the best in Asia by Finance Asia and the Asset Magazine for 3 years in a row. He has an MBA from the Indian Institute of Management Bangalore and a Bachelor of Technology from the Indian Institute of Technology (BHU) Varanasi, graduating as the gold medalist in both.
